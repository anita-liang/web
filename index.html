Origins

Lana Del Rey was born Elizabeth Wooldridge Grant in Lake Placid, New York. She moved to New York City and attended Fordham University for a degree in metaphysics and philosophy. During her time in New York, she became acquantined with Stefani Germaotta or "Lady Gaga." They ended up having a falling out but made up since then. While she had multiple psudenyoms throughout her career in which she released music under, such as "May Jailer" and "Lizzy Grant", she received the most success under "Lana Del Rey" with five albums at the moment.
